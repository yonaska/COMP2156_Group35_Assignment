George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Canada. The college offers a variety of programs across different fields like business, health sciences, and technology. It is known for its emphasis on experiential learning, preparing students for real-world challenges. George Brown is home to more than 30,000 students, and its strong industry connections help students gain valuable work experience during their studies.
George Brown College offers scholarships and financial aid to eligible students.
